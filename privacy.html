Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Life Budget Planner ("the App") is an offline-first personal finance utility. We designed the App so your budget data stays on your device.

Data we store

  • Projects, stages, expenses, planned expenses, notes, and app settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage.
  • Optional receipt photos you attach are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate user accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or advertising.

Permissions

  • Notifications (optional): Local budget alerts at 80% and when over budget.
  • Biometrics (optional): App lock using Face ID, Touch ID, or device biometrics.
  • Camera / Photos (optional): Attach receipt images to expenses.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Export actions (JSON backup, CSV, PDF, project share) only occur when you explicitly choose to share a file from your device.

Data retention & deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App. You can use Clear All Data in Settings to remove all local data.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
